    • Community

    We want all women to have the confidence and know-how to achieve their goals. This starts with an active and supportive community. We encourage an open exchange of ideas and information. We will also share Lean In Stories which are short narratives of moments in our members’ lives when we choose to “lean in” intended to inspire, teach and connect us.

    Education/Mentorship

    We offer an opportunity for our members to form bonds and mentorship relationships that help to advance personal and professional development and aspirations. We also offer workshops and other educational events to build skills and share experiences.

    Circles

    Lean In Circles are small groups that meet monthly to encourage and support each other in an atmosphere of confidentiality and trust. We provide the materials and support to run a successful Circle, including online spaces that make it easy for members to stay up-to-date and connected.
